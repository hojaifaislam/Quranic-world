The Islamic Comparative Religion Academy (ICRA) offers a Global specialist platform to impart information, knowledge, and training in Comparative Religion to Muslims and people of other Faiths as well. We participate with other faiths in symposia, dialogues and debates in a non-confrontational manner, in an exemplary behavior and with dignified conduct to spread the Message of Allah world-wide.

Our Mission

To Flood The World With The Message Of The Qur'an

Hounarable Sponsor’s

To empower students by facilitating improved educational and ecclesiastical skills in Comparative Religion.

To encourage and promote cordial relationships and co-operation among people of all Faiths.

To promote conferences and conventions with others whose deliberations seek to benefit humanity.

To participate in symposia, dialogue and debates with Missionaries in a constructive and meaningful manner.

Our Objectives

To promote and encourage research, education and training in the field of Comparative Religion.
